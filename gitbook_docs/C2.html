    • 3.5 Main Findings
      • 3.5.1 ‘What’: The majority of nitrogen in the Peel-Harvey is in organic forms
      • 3.5.2 ‘What’: Nutrients originating from soils and groundwaters will be difficult to track, but rain provides a distinct inorganic nitrogen source
      • 3.5.3 ‘Where’: Shallow, productive waters are nitrogen removal hot-spots
      • 3.5.4 ‘When’: Both the source and amount of nitrogen reaching the estuary fluctuates seasonally
      • 3.5.5 ‘Why’: Both natural streams and artificial drains remove significant quantities of nitrogen during transport towards the estuary
